# TamperTantrum Labs Goals

SMART goals for TamperTantrum Labs AppSec consulting business.

## Short-Term Goals (3-6 Months)

### 1. Complete 3 Paid Client Projects
- **Specific**: Book and deliver at least 3 paid projects, with at least one being a direct referral or follow-up from a free/discounted engagement
- **Measurable**: 3 completed paid engagements
- **Focus**: Revenue, trust-building, real-world experience
- **Status**: Not started

### 2. Launch a Live Website with Contact Flow
- **Specific**: Deploy a functional, branded website with a contact form, service list, and CTA
- **Measurable**: Website live and accessible
- **Focus**: Establish legitimacy, start capturing leads
- **Status**: Not started

### 3. Publish 3 LinkedIn Posts per Week
- **Specific**: Share 3 posts/week for 12 weeks (36 total), focused on AppSec insights, dev tips, tools, and project updates
- **Measurable**: 36 posts over 12 weeks
- **Focus**: Build visibility, authority, and content repurposing base
- **Status**: Not started

### 4. Refine & Test 2 Core Service Packages
- **Specific**: Define pricing, scope, and deliverables for 2 services (e.g., "AppSec Mini Audit" and "CI/CD Hardening") and test through real client work
- **Measurable**: 2 productized service packages defined
- **Focus**: Reduce custom scoping, increase sales clarity
- **Status**: Not started

### 5. Create a Portfolio Case Study
- **Specific**: Write and publish 1 in-depth case study (client or OSS project) to highlight process, findings, and results
- **Measurable**: 1 published case study
- **Focus**: Credibility builder for proposals, site, and outreach
- **Status**: Not started

### 6. Set Up Basic Client Ops Stack
- **Specific**: Create repeatable templates for onboarding, project tracking, reporting, and wrap-ups
- **Measurable**: Templates created in Notion/Trello/Google Docs
- **Focus**: Reduce friction, set foundation for scale
- **Status**: Not started
