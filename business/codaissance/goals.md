# Codaissance Goals

SMART goals for Codaissance product studio.

## Revenue Goals

### Milestone 1: First Revenue
- **Specific**: Generate first dollar of revenue from any Codaissance product
- **Measurable**: $1+ in revenue
- **Achievable**: Via MindTrace or other product MVP
- **Relevant**: Proves product-market fit exists
- **Status**: Not started

### Milestone 2: $1K MRR
- **Specific**: Reach $1,000 monthly recurring revenue
- **Measurable**: Track via payment processor dashboard
- **Relevant**: Validates business model, proves sustainable path
- **Status**: Not started

### Milestone 3: $5K MRR
- **Specific**: Reach $5,000 monthly recurring revenue
- **Measurable**: Track via payment processor dashboard
- **Relevant**: Meaningful income, validates portfolio approach
- **Status**: Not started

### Milestone 4: $10K MRR
- **Specific**: Reach $10,000 monthly recurring revenue
- **Measurable**: Track via payment processor dashboard
- **Relevant**: Evaluate reducing employment hours
- **Status**: Not started

## Product Goals

### Launch MVP
- **Specific**: Ship first product MVP (Dev Genesis or MindTrace)
- **Measurable**: Product live and accessible to users
- **Relevant**: Can't generate revenue without a product
- **Status**: In progress

### Second Product
- **Specific**: Launch second product in portfolio
- **Measurable**: Product live with distinct user base
- **Relevant**: Validates product studio model
- **Status**: Not started
