# Business Goals

High-level objectives across Codaissance and TamperTantrum Labs.

## Ultimate Vision

Build sustainable, independent businesses that:
1. Replace employment income through product revenue
2. Provide financial security and flexibility
3. Allow working on meaningful problems without corporate constraints
4. Create tools that respect users and prioritize quality over growth metrics

1. **Build in Public** - Transparency builds trust and audience
2. **Security-First** - Both businesses benefit from security expertise
3. **Quality Over Speed** - Sustainable growth, not growth hacking
4. **Independence** - No VC pressure, no compromising values for metrics

## Success Metrics

### Short-term (6 months)
- Ship 1-2 Codaissance MVPs
- TamperTantrum Labs website live
- Consistent LinkedIn posting across all 3 accounts
- 5+ job applications submitted (backup plan)

### Medium-term (12 months)
- Codaissance generating any recurring revenue
- TamperTantrum Labs with steady client work
- Audience growth across platforms

### Long-term (24+ months)
- Combined business revenue approaching/exceeding day job salary
- Option to reduce employment or go full-time on businesses
