## Success Metrics

### Short-term (6 months)
- Ship 1-2 MVPs
- Consistent content creation
- Build initial audience

### Medium-term (12 months)
- Generating recurring revenue
- Steady growth trajectory
- Audience engagement

### Long-term (24+ months)
- Business revenue approaching/exceeding day job salary
- Option to reduce employment or go full-time on business
