## Compensation Requirements

### Minimum
- **Salary**: $90,000/year (current is $70k, need meaningful increase to justify switch)
- **Benefits**: Health insurance required

### Target
- **Salary**: $120,000 - $200,000/year
- **Equity**: Nice to have, not required
- **Bonus**: Nice to have

### Would Consider Lower For
- Exceptional learning opportunity
- Clear path to senior/lead role
- Strong equity in early-stage startup with traction

## Work Preferences

### Location
- **Remote**: Strongly preferred
- **Hybrid**: Would consider for right opportunity (must be reasonable commute)
- **On-site**: Only for exceptional comp or opportunity - not preferred and probably not possible given current life situation, wife's businss, and area of residence. (Portsmouth, OH)

### Company Type
- **Preferred**: Startup or mid-size company with growth potential
- **Open to**: Enterprise if team/product is interesting
- **Avoid**: Agencies, body shops, consulting firms (want to build products)

### Team & Culture
- Want to write code daily
- Collaborative environment
- Tech stack doesn't matter as long as it fits my skills and growth goals
- Emphasis on work-life balance
- Some autonomy in technical decisions

## Dealbreakers
- Salary below $90,000
- No health insurance
- Outdated tech stack (jQuery, PHP without modern framework, etc.)
- Pure support/customer-facing role disguised as engineering
- Mandatory on-site 5 days/week
- Contract/1099 without significant premium
