## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (January 1-15, 2026)

**Goals:**
- Build email list of 200+ interested developers
- Create anticipation and social proof
- Validate messaging

**Actions:**

### Lead Magnet
Free "AI Context Guide" - How to make AI tools understand your full context
- PDF or Notion doc
- Captures email for launch notification
- Demonstrates expertise

### Content Blitz
- 3 LinkedIn posts per week about the problem/solution
- 1 longer-form piece (blog or LinkedIn article)
- Daily engagement in dev communities

### Landing Page
- Simple page with value prop, email capture
- "Be first to know when Professional OS launches"
- Show what's coming (screenshots, feature list)
- **Tease the dashboard:** "Coming soon: hosted dashboard with auto-apply"

### Community Seeding
- Share insights in relevant Discord/Slack communities
- Don't sell—educate and help
- Build reputation as someone who solved this problem

## Messaging Framework

### Primary Message
"Stop re-explaining yourself to AI. Your professional identity, version-controlled."

### Supporting Messages
1. **For Job Seekers:** "Generate tailored resumes in 5 minutes, not 5 hours"
2. **For Builders:** "Your professional brand on autopilot"
3. **For AI Users:** "Make Claude/Copilot 10x more useful with full context"
4. **For Dashboard:** "See your career data. Track your progress. Apply automatically."

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I can build this myself" | "You could. But will you? And will you maintain it? This is ready now." |
| "It's just files and folders" | "Structure is the value. Plus automations, commands, and hosted dashboard." |
| "Too expensive" | "How many hours do you spend on resumes and context? $97 pays for itself in one job application." |
| "I don't use AI tools that much" | "You will. And when you do, your context will be ready." |
| "Why pay for the dashboard?" | "Private repo access, custom subdomain, and auto-apply to jobs. The free tier is forever free." |
