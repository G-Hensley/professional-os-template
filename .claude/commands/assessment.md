## Assessment Structure

Each assessment will consist of the following components:

1. **Review**: A thorough review of my skills, projects, and performance in the specified areas. This should be listed out in section format with headings.
2. **Feedback**: Constructive feedback highlighting strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement.
3. **Recommendations**: Actionable recommendations for enhancing my skills, project management, marketing strategies, and overall performance.
4. **Resources**: Suggested resources such as courses, books, tools, communities, articles, tutorials, and documentation to support my development.
5. **Follow-up**: A plan for follow-up assessments to track progress and adjust strategies as needed.
6. **Summary**: A concise summary of the assessment findings and next steps, checklist of action items, and when the next assessment should take place.

## Assessment Guidelines

- Assessments should be objective, evidence-based, and tailored to my specific goals and context.
- Use a combination of qualitative and quantitative data to support evaluations and recommendations.
- Ensure confidentiality and professionalism in handling personal and project information.
- Encourage a growth mindset, focusing on continuous improvement and learning opportunities.
- Suggest realistic and achievable goals based on my current skills and resources.
- Provide clear and actionable steps for implementing recommendations.
- Suggest when the next assessment should take place based on progress and development needs.
- Create a markdown file for each assessment with the date and area of focus in the filename (e.g., `2025-12-18-assessment.md`) within the [assessments directory](../../assessments).
- Use headings, bullet points, and tables to organize information clearly and concisely.
- Include links to relevant resources, tools, and documentation to support learning and development.
- Review and update the assessment guidelines periodically to ensure they remain relevant and effective.

## Assessment Types

### Full Assessment
Covers all areas comprehensively. Recommended monthly.

### Focused Assessment
Covers a specific area in depth. Use filename format: `YYYY-MM-DD-{area}-assessment.md`
- `technical-assessment.md` - Technical skills only
- `business-assessment.md` - Business acumen and strategy
- `marketing-assessment.md` - Marketing and growth
- `career-assessment.md` - Resume, portfolio, job search
- `financial-assessment.md` - Revenue, expenses, progress to goals

### Quick Check
A brief status check on key metrics without full analysis. Useful for weekly reviews.
