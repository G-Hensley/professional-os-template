# Assessment Guide

This document provides guidelines for conducting assessments of me - Gavin Hensley. These assessments will evaluate my skills, knowledge, overall performance, and improvement areas from business, tech, personal development, and other relevant perspectives.

## Assessment Areas
- **Technical Skills**: Evaluate my proficiency in programming languages, frameworks, tools, and technologies listed in my [skills profile](../../profile/skills.json).
- **Project Management**: Assess my ability to plan, execute, and deliver projects effectively, including time management, resource allocation, and risk management. Find this in [my projects](../../projects/active.json) and [planned projects](../../projects/planned.json).
- **Marketing & Growth**: Review my strategies and effectiveness in promoting projects, engaging users, and driving growth, including SEO, social media, and content marketing. To also suggest improvements. Assess this from the perspective of my [businesses](../../business).
- **Business Acumen**: Evaluate my understanding of business concepts, market trends, and customer needs relevant to my projects and career goals. Review this from the perspective of my [businesses](../../business).
- **Resume & Portfolio**: Evaluate the quality and relevance of my resume and portfolio in showcasing my skills and projects, and suggest improvements. Review my [experience](../../profile/experience.json), [active projects](../../projects/active.json), [completed projects](../../projects/completed.json), [skills](../../profile/skills.json), and [education](../../profile/education.json).
- **Job Search Strategies**: Assess my methods for finding and applying to job opportunities, including networking and interview preparation, and suggestions for improvement. Review my [applications](../../jobs/applications.json) and [interviews](../../jobs/interviews.json).
- **Idea Generation**: Evaluate my ability to generate innovative ideas for projects, products, or solutions, and suggest techniques to enhance creativity. Review my [ideas folder](../../ideas).
- **Personal Development**: Review my commitment to continuous learning, skill development, and self-improvement.

## Assessment Structure
Each assessment will consist of the following components:
1. **Review**: A thorough review of my skills, projects, and performance in the specified areas. This should be listed out in section format with headings.
2. **Feedback**: Constructive feedback highlighting strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement.
3. **Recommendations**: Actionable recommendations for enhancing my skills, project management, marketing strategies, and overall performance.
4. **Resources**: Suggested resources such as courses, books, tools, communities, tools, articles, tutorials, and documentation to support my development, learning, resumes, marketiing, and job search efforts.
5. **Follow-up**: A plan for follow-up assessments to track progress and adjust strategies as needed.
6. **Summary**: A concise summary of the assessment findings and next steps, checklist of action items, timeline for implementation, and when the next assessment should take place.

## Assessment Guidelines
- Assessments should be objective, evidence-based, and tailored to my specific goals and context.
- Use a combination of qualitative and quantitative data to support evaluations and recommendations.
- Ensure confidentiality and professionalism in handling personal and project information.
- Encourage a growth mindset, focusing on continuous improvement and learning opportunities.
- Suggest realistic and achievable goals based on my current skills and resources.
- Provide clear and actionable steps for implementing recommendations.
- Suggest when the next assessment should take place based on progress and development needs.
- Create a markdown file for each assessment with the date and area of focus in the filename (e.g., `2025-12-17-assessment.md`) within the [assessments directory](../../assessments).
- Use headings, bullet points, and tables to organize information clearly and concisely.
- Include links to relevant resources, tools, and documentation to support learning and development. (Check the links are valid and working.)
- Review and update the assessment guidelines periodically to ensure they remain relevant and effective.
